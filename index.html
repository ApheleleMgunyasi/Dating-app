Dating Terms & Conditions

Welcome to my pre-dating portal. Before engaging in any romantic trial, please review the terms below carefully. These conditions reflect my standards, expectations, and how I choose to be respected in a potential relationship.

Eligibility Requirements

  1. You must have completed high school and currently be enrolled in tertiary, university, or college studies — or have earned a bachelor’s qualification.
  2. You must not have a child.
  3. You must not have a girlfriend or be involved in any romantic relationship at the present time.
  4. You must be between the ages of 18 and 30.
  5. You must be a South African citizen or have a valid work permit.
  6. You must be able to communicate fluently in English.
  7. You must be willing to meet in person for a date within the next month.
  8. You must not have criminal record

Relationship Expectations

  • Girlfriend allowance must be provided — consistency appreciated.
  • Daily communication is non-negotiable: texts, calls, voice notes, or any loving check-in.
  • Celebration of Valentine’s Day and my birthday is mandatory — thoughtful gestures count!
  • Vacations are part of my love language. Let's plan relaxing, romantic getaways together.
